faq

1. Who is behind this?

ans- r001 is the assembler of this project. The reason for starting this project can be read in the about section.

2. I rkmnd'ed a resource but it is not showing?

Ans- If your resource fulfills all the requirements(cc, free to use etc.) then kindly wait for the weekend as changes are usually updated after every weekend. If still not showing, drop me an email .

3. I rkmnd'ed a resource but someone else' name is showing as rkmnd'er?

Ans- First of all thank you for rkmnd'ing a resource and sorry for your name not as rkmnd'er. Since many people rkmnd the same resource so only those who first rkmnd'ed it with testimonials are named as rkmnd'er (First come first get). However your rkmnd'tion is counted and your testimonial may be shown.

4. I knew a resource and I wanted to rkmnd it but it has already been rkmnd'ed by someone.

Ans-Chances are you can still claim that resource only if it was originally submitted by r001 without a testimonial. Claim by writing a testimonial for it. Kindly also provide either your name&contact or write as anonymous.

5. Tips for writing testimonials.

ans- You can write as how a resource helped you, what you loved about it. Just keep it natural and simple !
